Tastings

with Soo Jin

"What do you want to eat today?" is a hard question for me to answer because there are just so many options to choose from, especially in NYC, where I live. When I'm trying to decide on what to eat, I like to look at pictures of foods rather than just the reviews, and so I've created this photography blog on the dishes that I've tried and loved. I hope my blog can be a friendly website you can visit whenever you are lost trying to decide what to eat.
