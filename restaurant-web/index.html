Cafe Baci

Long Island's most loved Italian Restaurant!

Right on the heart of Nassau County, Cafe Baci has proudly served for over 25 years. Our casual and warm atmosphere has welcomed families, small/large groups, events, parties with excellent serivce and dining experience. We offer a delectable blend of authentic Roman cuisine with homemade Italian favorites. We also offer catering with custom menus and party planning and arrangement upon request.
