Tastings

with Soo Jin

"What do you want to eat today?" is one of the hardest questions for me to answer.There are just so many dishes and places to choose from, especially in NYC, where I live. When I'm trying to decided on what to eat, I like to look at pictures of foods rather than just the reviews, and so I've created this photography blog on the dishes that I've tried and loved. I hope my blog can be a friendly website you can visit whenever you are lost trying to decide what to eat.
