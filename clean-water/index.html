CLEAN WATER

Would you drink this water?

One of the first things you probably did this morning was use water. But about 1 in 9 people worldwide live without access to clean water. That's about twice the population of the United States.

In developing countries, access to clean water can change just about everything. In Africa, people spend about 40 billion hours a year walking to collect water, especially women and girls. That's about 6 hours a day fetching water. Clean water can allow women to spend their time farming to feed and raise income for their families and give girls the chance to attend school.
